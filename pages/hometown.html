Rebutan Gunungan tradition

This tradition is carried out simultaneously by the people of Bojonegoro. As the name implies, this tradition is in the form of a procession of agricultural products formed by mountains.

Ider ider uluk salam

Before the Bengawan festival, the people of Bojonegoro carry out the tradition of ider-ider uluk salam, which begins with a cone from Bendung Gerak to Taman Bengawan Solo. This tradition is quite unique because it is only followed by men. Then after that the Bengawan festival is held which can be followed by all walks of life.

Nyadran, sedekah bumi tradition

The nyadran tradition, or better known as alms of the earth, is where people flock to parading the produce and several types of food, including tumpeng, to go around the village. This tradition is a form of gratitude expressed by the village community to God Almighty. This tradition ends with the community gathering in the field and enjoying the food that has been paraded around the village.
